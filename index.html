Northmannish Dictionary

Northmannish is a new conlang for northerners in Aldraheimr 2nd Edition, created by Oneo64. This language is mostly derived from Old West Norse (unlike Oddarish), with sound change rules, irregularity rules, orthographical changes, etc. The primary goal is to give it an archaic and ancient feeling while making it different from other Germanic languages.

Kaldi vetrinn er nær, snæstormr mun koma. Komaþu inn varma húsit mitt, vinrinn minn. Velkominn! Komaþu herveg, syngjaþu ok danzaþu, etaþu ok sjúpaþu. Þat er vitlistin mín. Vit hefum vatn, øl, ok mjúlg sem fersk ór kúnni. Ó, ok varman ketilrett!

The cold winter is near, a snowstorm will come. Come in my warm house, my friend. Welcome! Come here, sing and dance, eat and drink. That is my plan. We have water, beer, and milk fresh from the cow. Oh, and warm soup!
